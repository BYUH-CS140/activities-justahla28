Climate change includes both global warming driven by human-induced emissions of greenhouse gases

and the resulting large-scale shifts in weather patterns.

Though there have been previous periods of change,

since the mid 20th century humans have had an unprecedented impact on Earth's climate

system and caused change on a global scale.

These changes pose significant risks to biodiversity, human health, and global economies.
