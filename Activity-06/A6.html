RECIPES

Burrito Bowls

Ingredients

  • ½ cup precooked or frozen brown rice to quinoa
  • ½ cup cooked black beans
  • ⅛ teaspoon cumin
  • ⅛ teaspoon cayenne
  • ⅛ teaspoon garlic powder
  • ½ cup red cabbage, shredded
  • 3 ounces shredded or chopped leftover chicken or extra-firm tofu
  • ⅓ avocado, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons fresh salsa

Instructions

  1. If using frozen rice, cook it according to the package instructions. If you're using precooked rice, set it aside for now—you'll add it to the beans in step 2 to reheat together.
  2. Place the black beans in a microwave-safe bowl. Add your preferred spices and a splash of water. If you're using precooked rice, add it to the bowl with the beans. Microwave on high for 30 to 45 seconds until heated through.
  3. While the beans are heating, slice or shred the cabbage. Cook or warm up the chicken or tofu. Slice the avocado and get your salsa ready.
  4. In a serving dish, start by layering the rice. Then add the beans, followed by the cabbage, chicken or tofu, and avocado.
  5. Top the bowl with salsa. Add any optional extras like lime juice, hot sauce, or fresh herbs if desired, then serve and enjoy.
